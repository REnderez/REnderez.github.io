Prêt-à-porter

Cheeky designer clothing for women and men, ethically made with love in the Philippines. A TC grrrl or #TCspecies are urban dwellers with a love for cultures of the world and whatever lowbrow culture means for you. Always with a sense of humour, a sense of adventure and whimsy, the Wearer of Tipay Caintic lives a life in full technicolour.

For the Love of Leyte Music & Art Festival

Capsule Collection

PHL 2017:

There is no longer a distinction between art and the life it imitates, maybe there never was. Fashion is not apathetic. It is most sensitive and susceptible to a nation’s most idiotic mistakes. Who makes the clothes, who wears them, who dreams of the new things? Who sells, who takes advantage, who makes a pretty profit? How unfortunate we all live under the same government, the president’s government.
