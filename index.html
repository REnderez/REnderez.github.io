TIPAY THE DESIGNER

Tipay Caintic is a creative and fashion entrepreneur from the Philippines. She is based in Manila and frequently flies to her hometown Tacloban City in the province of Leyte for private clients and projects with For the Love of Leyte (FLOL), a non-profit organisation she co-founded in 2018 with fellow creatives and Leytenons.

Prêt-à-porter

Cheeky designer clothing for women and men, ethically made with love in the Philippines. SOCIALLY DRIVEN DESIGN WORK

Purposeful design work that aims to improve the quality of life for fellow Filipinos, makers of our clothing and partner artisans. The projects undertaken under this ethos look to improve production practices, refinement of skill, pride in the work we do and overall economic growth in our creative industries.
